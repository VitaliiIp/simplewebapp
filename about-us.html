About Us

At Lingvex, we organize the translation process to be clear and efficient — from the first request to final delivery.

View Services

Once you submit your project through our platform, we review the details, confirm requirements, and assign a specialist with relevant subject expertise. Pricing and turnaround times are determined individually, depending on the language pair, complexity, and field.

Our services go beyond written translation. We provide phone and video interpreting, voice-over recording, subtitling, proofreading, foreign-language typesetting, and audio transcription. Lingvex focuses on non-notarized translations including website content, social media materials, educational resources, and creative texts. For legal or officially binding use, requirements must be discussed and confirmed in advance.

Quality control is built into every stage of our workflow. Translators are selected based on experience and subject knowledge, and each project undergoes structured review before delivery. Rely on Lingvex to deliver language solutions that seamlessly bridge global communication.

Partnership Opportunities

For translators looking to grow their careers with us.

The first step is to register in the system as a translator. After you register and your information is verified, a team member will contact you to schedule an interview. During the interview, our senior specialists assess your skills using industry-approved language proficiency tests (writing, listening, speaking, and reading).

If everything goes well, your personal account will be created in the system, where you will receive documents for translation. A specific rate will be assigned to you for each translated document. You can withdraw the money earned at any time to any of your Visa or Mastercard cards.

Our partnerships go beyond collaboration, focusing on long-term mutual growth.

Questions & Answers

The most common questions about working with Lingvex.

What is Lingvex?

Lingvex is a professional translation company offering written translation, interpreting, and localization services across 20+ languages, supported by industry-specialist linguists and a structured quality-review process.

What services does Lingvex offer?

Written translation, phone and video interpreting, voice-over recording, subtitling, proofreading, foreign-language typesetting, and audio transcription. We work across legal, technical, marketing, and corporate domains.

What types of translations do you focus on?

Lingvex focuses on non-notarized translations — website content, social media materials, educational resources, creative texts, and business documents. Officially binding translations are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Can I use translations from Lingvex for legal purposes?

For legal or officially binding use, requirements must be discussed and confirmed in advance. Contact our team before placing the order so we can match you with the appropriate specialist.

How are translation orders processed?

You submit the project through our platform. We review the details, confirm requirements, and assign a specialist with relevant subject expertise. The translated document is reviewed before delivery.

What factors affect the price and delivery time?

Word count, language pair, subject complexity, and your selected service level (Economy, Professional, or Premium). Additional services like proofreading or formatting can also influence the total.

How does Lingvex ensure the quality of translations?

Translators are selected based on experience and subject knowledge, glossaries are followed where provided, and every project passes through structured review by a specialized reviser before delivery.

How do I place an order?

Use the contact page or pricing page to request a quote. Once we confirm the scope, you'll receive a final price and turnaround estimate before work begins.
